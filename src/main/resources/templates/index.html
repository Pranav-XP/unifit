Frequently Asked Questions

Our advanced matching system considers your skill level, playing style, location, and availability to connect you with compatible teammates. We use a rating system and player preferences to ensure balanced and enjoyable matchups.

We offer access to various court types including indoor gymnasiums, outdoor street courts, and professional complexes. Each venue is verified for quality and safety, and many include amenities like lighting, seating, and water stations.

We offer both free and premium membership tiers. Basic membership is free and includes court finding and basic team matching. Premium members get additional features like priority matching, advanced stats tracking, and exclusive event access.

Simply select your preferred court, date, and time, then specify how many players you need. Our system will notify compatible players in your area. Once enough players accept, the game is automatically confirmed, and all participants receive court details and teammate information.
